CAUSE OF DEATH

IN MALAYSIA (2022)

Since 2018, the Ministry of Health Malaysia (MoH) has implemented a process to verify non-medically certified causes of death to increase percentage of deaths that are medically certified. In 2022, 61.1% of 206,525 recorded deaths were medically certified, while 38.9% were not.

The Department of Statistics Malaysia (DOSM) follows the International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems, 10th Revision (ICD-10), published by the World Health Organization, to categorize causes of death. These causes are classified as Medically Certified, where verification is done by medical officers or coroners, and Non-medically Certified, where verification is conducted by informants without medical qualifications, such as individuals or the police.
